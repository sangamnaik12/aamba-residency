About Us

A Premier Hotel in Sundargarh

Aamba Residency is Sundargarh's trusted destination for comfortable and quality accommodation. Located in Aamba Tower on Hospital Road, we offer 20 well-appointed, air-conditioned rooms designed to make you feel right at home.

Whether you're here for business, leisure, or a family trip, our round-the-clock service, multi-cuisine dining, and thoughtful amenities ensure a memorable stay every time.

  • 20 clean, spacious, air-conditioned rooms
  • Multi-cuisine restaurant on premises
  • Complimentary buffet breakfast every morning
  • Conference & banquet facilities
  • Airport transfer & laundry service
  • Staff speak English, Hindi & Odia
